Savory Oatmeal

Prep: 8 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Serving: 3

01.

Boil water (or broth) with oats in a small saucepan, stirring occasionally until thick and creamy.

02.

Stir in chopped spinach while in saucepan.

03.

Take off heat and add squash, spices, and salt and pepper to taste.

04.

Top with fried egg, if desired, and serve warm.
